Quality Materials

We use trusted suppliers and premium materials so your project stands the test of time.

At Rendelco we never cut corners on materials. Every project uses products from our approved supplier list, with warranties and documentation on request. We specify grades that meet or exceed local building codes and work with you to balance durability, appearance, and budget.

Our approach

  • Preferred partnerships with national and regional suppliers for bulk pricing and consistent supply.
  • All structural and envelope materials are sourced from certified or accredited manufacturers.
  • Finishes (flooring, tiles, fixtures) are selected from vetted ranges so quality is predictable.
  • We keep samples and data sheets on file for key items so you can review choices before installation.
