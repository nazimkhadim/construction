Safety Standards

Strict safety protocols on every site to protect our team, your property, and the public.

Safety is non-negotiable. We follow site-specific risk assessments, use PPE and barriers where required, and keep access and signage clear. Our team is trained on current regulations and we work with clients to minimise disruption while keeping everyone safe.

Our commitment

  • Pre-start safety briefings and toolbox talks so everyone knows the rules for that site.
  • Correct PPE, fencing, and signage in place before work begins and maintained throughout.
  • Incident reporting and follow-up so we learn and improve.
  • Compliance with local health and safety requirements and, where applicable, client safety systems.
